Why This Topic Matters

Artificial intelligence is changing the way people work. Some jobs may become easier because AI can handle repetitive tasks, organize information, and support decision-making. At the same time, many workers worry that automation could replace certain positions or make it harder for people to compete in the job market.

This page explores how AI may affect employment, which types of jobs could be most exposed to automation, and why workers may need to build new skills as technology continues to improve.

Major Effects of AI on Jobs

  1. Jobs that may be more exposed to automation
    • Repetitive office and clerical tasks
    • Basic data entry and document processing
    • Some customer service and support roles
  2. Skills that may become more valuable
    • Critical thinking and problem solving
    • Communication and leadership
    • Technology, analytics, and AI literacy
  3. Possible benefits of AI in the workplace
    • Higher productivity
    • Faster research and analysis
    • Support for workers instead of full replacement
